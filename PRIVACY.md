# Privacy Policy - The Anti-List

**Last updated:** March 9, 2026

## Overview
The Anti-List is a simple productivity app that allows only one task at a time. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection
The Anti-List does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any servers.

## Local Storage
Your task is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

## Third-Party Services
The Anti-List does not use any third-party services, analytics, tracking, or advertising.
